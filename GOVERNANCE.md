\# Governance



WFSL Evidence Guard defines deterministic evidence emission and handling.



\## Scope

\- Evidence is generated deterministically

\- Evidence is append-only

\- Evidence is machine-verifiable



\## Non-claims

\- No legal conclusions

\- No compliance certification

\- No human judgement replacement



\## Change control

\- Evidence formats must remain backward compatible

\- Breaking changes require new verification

\- Evidence removal is forbidden



\## Elevation readiness

Eligible for WFSL verification when:

\- Apache 2.0 licence is present

\- Verification artefact exists

\- Deterministic verification runner passes
