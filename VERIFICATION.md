\# Verification



Verification confirms deterministic evidence emission and integrity.



\## Verification intent

\- Evidence is reproducible

\- Evidence structure is stable

\- Evidence files are immutable once written



\## Verification scope

This verification does not assert:

\- correctness of external inputs

\- legal admissibility

\- completeness of evidence sets



\## Minimum verification requirements

WFSL verification requires:

1\. Apache License 2.0 detected

2\. Governance artefacts present

3\. Deterministic verification runner passes

4\. Evidence artefact committed
